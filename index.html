SURVIVE IT

Let's See How Long You Can Survive

Rules
  • Use keyboard arrow keys or number keys(2,4,6,7)to move user and stay away from white squares.
  • Collect yellow squares to get points.
  • After every level the white squares speeds up.
  • After every 5 points another white square will follow user until another 3 points are taken.
